Overview

In this homework I implemented a rasterizer that can render vector graphics. Some of the available functionalities are point/line/triangle rasterization, supersampling (to prevent aliasing), texture mapping with nearest/bilinear sampling and level sampling, and transformations such as rotations and translations. I learned a lot about the rasterization pipeline, and enjoyed the opportunity to get my hands dirty with it. A lot of the pipeline was a black box to me, and most of it isnâ€™t now. I found it interesting to see the tradeoffs between different sampling methods, and I really enjoyed creating my own transforms.
